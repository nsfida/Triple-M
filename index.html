Overview

Triple M by NSF (Money Management Module) is a comprehensive financial tracking application designed and developed by Nadeem Shahzad Fida. This powerful tool helps individuals and businesses manage their finances with precision and ease.

The application provides a secure cloud-based solution with server storage for expenses, loans, and goods transactions, ensuring data safety and accessibility. Additionally, it offers cryptocurrency management through Bitcoin wallet integration with local backup options.

Enterprise Security & Infrastructure

Separate Database for Each User

Each user/company/merchant gets a dedicated, isolated database ensuring complete data separation and enhanced security. Your financial data remains private and inaccessible to other users.

AES-256 Security Encryption

Military-grade AES-256 encryption protects your login credentials and sensitive data, making it nearly impossible for unauthorized access without the actual password.

Multi-Currency Support (5 Currencies)

Comprehensive support for AED, SAR, PKR, USD, and BTC with real-time conversion rates and separate wallet management for each currency.

Advanced Financial Features

Expense Tracking

Comprehensive expense management with categorization, multiple account tracking, and detailed reporting across all supported currencies.

Loans Management

Complete loan tracking system for loans given and received, with payment schedules, interest calculations, and automated reminders.

Shop Inventory Tracking

Advanced inventory management system for tracking stock levels, sales, purchases, and profit margins with automatic reorder alerts.

Installment Tracking

Manage installment plans with flexible payment schedules, partial payment tracking, and automated balance calculations.

Bitcoin Blockchain Integration

Full Bitcoin wallet functionality with blockchain connectivity, live transaction broadcasting, wallet creation, and paper wallet download capabilities.

About Triple M by NSF

Learn more about the Money Management Module and its features.

Overview

Triple M by NSF (Money Management Module) is a comprehensive financial tracking application designed and developed by Nadeem Shahzad Fida. This powerful tool helps individuals and businesses manage their finances with precision and ease.

The application provides a secure cloud-based solution with server storage for expenses, loans, and goods transactions, ensuring data safety and accessibility. Additionally, it offers cryptocurrency management through Bitcoin wallet integration with local backup options.

Key Features

Expense Management

Track daily expenses across multiple wallets with detailed categorization and balance monitoring. Supports multiple currencies (AED, SAR, PKR) with real-time conversion and comprehensive reporting.

Loan Tracking

Manage loans given and received with detailed payment tracking, proper calculations, and repayment schedules. Monitor active loans and closed transactions with comprehensive filtering options.

Installment Plans

Create and manage installment plans with flexible payment schedules. Track partial payments and remaining balances with automatic status updates.

Goods Management

Track bought and sold items with inventory management. Monitor stock levels, sales records, and profit margins with detailed transaction history.

Bitcoin Integration

Secure Bitcoin wallet functionality with client-side key management. Create, import, and manage Bitcoin wallets with transaction history and balance tracking. All operations remain private and secure in your browser.

Security & Privacy

Secure Server Storage: Your financial data (expenses, loans, goods) is securely stored on our protected servers with enterprise-grade encryption and regular backups, ensuring data safety and accessibility from any device.

Bitcoin Privacy: Bitcoin wallet operations remain completely client-side. Private keys never leave your browser, providing full control over your cryptocurrency assets.

Local Backup Options: Export your complete financial data in JSON or CSV format for local backup and offline access. Import functionality allows seamless data restoration and migration.
